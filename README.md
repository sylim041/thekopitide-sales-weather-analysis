# thekopitide-sales-weather-analysis

📊The Kopi Tide Sales & Weather Analysis

📌 Business Problem
To identify and analyze top-performing products, peak sales periods, seasonal weather impacts, and customer preferences, so that ‘The Kopi Tide’ can make data-driven decisions to optimize operations, improve customer satisfaction, and maximize revenue growth.

📂 Dataset

Sales transactions (April–May 2024)
Weather data (rainfall, temperature, wind speed)

🛠 Tools Used

SQL
Excel / Python
Tableau

📈 Key Insights

Discovered ~5% drop in CBD sales and ~7% drop in heartlands sales on rainy days, indicating weather sensitivity.
The CBD outlet demonstrates a stronger preference for cashless payments compared to heartland outlets, likely reflecting differences in customer profiles such as working professionals versus residential shoppers.
Heartland outlet sees a spike in sales during the weekend due to increased residential footfall, whereas CBD outlet transactions decline as the weekday working crowd is absent during weekends.
Identified Teh C and Kopi O as the best-performing beverages, highlighting a clear customer preference for traditional drinks. This presents an opportunity for menu promotions to drive further sales.

💡 Business Recommendations

Introduce rainy-day promotions and delivery options to optimize sales on rainy days.
Focus on weekend campaigns for heartland outlets
To encourage cashless payments for heartland outlet to improve efficiency and reduce emphasis on cash handling.
Increase staffing on weekdays, reduce on weekends for CBD outlet.
Introduce quick lunch deals to target office workers for CBD outlet during weekdays.
